Dr. Linjun Yang has over 10 years experiences on researching/implementing cutting-edge image retrieval/computer vision/machine learning technologies. He has authored/co-authored more than 60 peer-reviewed papers published in various conference proceedings and journals and 23 filed US/international patents. His papers have gotten over 2000 citations and his h-index is 22 according to Google Scholar 4/2015. He is now leading a team to develop various advanced image understanding technologies/systems on billions of Web image data, including duplicate-detection, similarity search, annotation, etc., with application to various Microsoft products.
